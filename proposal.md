# 🏛️ Hybrid Token Launch Proposal for Pi Network

---

## 📌 Introduction
This proposal introduces a **hybrid token launch model** for the Pi ecosystem that balances:

- 💧 **Liquidity Pools** — ensure market stability  
- 🔄 **Developer Incentives** — reward responsible project development  
- 🛡️ **Ecosystem Reserve** — protect participants

---

## 🎯 Motivation
Pi Network requires a sustainable model to:

- ✅ Ensure strong market liquidity  
- ✅ Reward developers responsibly  
- ✅ Protect ecosystem participants

---

## 💰 Token Allocation
| Category | Percentage | Purpose |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| 💧 Liquidity Pools | 65% | Market stability & liquidity |
| 🔄 Developers | 20% | Milestone-based development incentives |
| 🛡️ Ecosystem Reserve | 15% | Protect ecosystem & fund emergencies |

---

## 🛡️ Reserve Mechanism
- Gradually decreases over time (15% → 10% → 5%)  
- Redistributed proportionally between:
  - Liquidity Pools  
  - Developer Allocations

---

## 👨‍💻 Developer Incentives
- Tokens released based on milestones  
- Encourages long-term, responsible project development

---

## ⚖️ Enforcement
- Fraud, rug pulls, or abandonment → permanent ban from ecosystem

---

## 🌟 Benefits
- Strong liquidity and market stability  
- Fair developer incentives  
- Ecosystem protection and sustainable growth  
- Transparent and innovation-friendly environment
