# Penalties and Slashing Mechanism

## Overview
To ensure accountability and protect the ecosystem, the hybrid token model introduces a penalty and slashing system.

Participants that violate ecosystem rules may face financial or governance penalties.

## Penalty Types

### 1. Reward Suspension
If a project fails to meet milestones:
- Developer rewards may be temporarily suspended.

### 2. Allocation Reduction
If suspicious activity is detected:
- A portion of the developer allocation may be reduced.

### 3. Slashing
In severe cases such as fraud or rug pulls:
- Part of the project’s allocated tokens may be permanently removed.

Example:

Developer allocation = 200,000 tokens  
Fraud detected → 50% slashed

Remaining allocation = 100,000 tokens

### 4. Permanent Ecosystem Ban
Projects proven to intentionally harm participants may be permanently excluded from the ecosystem.

## Governance Role

Governance mechanisms can:

- Investigate suspected abuse
- Vote on enforcement actions
- Protect the ecosystem from malicious actors
