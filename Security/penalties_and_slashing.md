## Overview
To ensure accountability and protect the ecosystem, the hybrid token model introduces a **dynamic penalty and slashing system**.

Participants that violate ecosystem rules may face **financial, governance, or reputation penalties**.

## Penalty Types

### 1. Reward Suspension
- Developer rewards may be temporarily suspended if milestones are not met.
- Suspension events are logged on-chain for transparency.

### 2. Allocation Reduction
- Portion of developer allocation may be reduced in case of suspicious activity.
- Reduction is proportional to severity and history.
- Reputation score is decreased accordingly.

### 3. Slashing
- Severe cases such as fraud or rug pulls can trigger slashing.
- Slashing percentage is dynamic:
  - Minor fraud → 10-20%
  - Major fraud → 50-70%
  - Repeated violations → 100% + permanent ban
- Each slashing is logged as an **on-chain event**.

### 4. Permanent Ecosystem Ban
- Projects proven to intentionally harm participants may be permanently excluded.
- Governance voting required for permanent ban decision.

## Governance Role
- Investigate suspected abuse
- Vote on enforcement actions (weighted by trust & reputation)
- Protect the ecosystem from malicious actors
