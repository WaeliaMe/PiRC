# Fraud Detection Framework

## Purpose
To protect the ecosystem, mechanisms are required to detect malicious behavior early.

## Examples of Fraud

- False profit reporting
- Rug pulls
- Artificial liquidity manipulation
- Project abandonment after receiving rewards

## Detection Methods

Fraud detection may involve:

- Governance community review
- On-chain activity monitoring
- Cross-verification of reported revenue
- Ecosystem audits

## Response Process

1. Suspicious activity is detected
2. Governance review is initiated
3. Investigation takes place
4. Enforcement action may follow
